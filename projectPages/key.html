KeyShot Rendering

KEYSHOT

is a rendering program that I learned Northwestern class. Our final assignment was to chose a model of our own and produce two renderings of it using KeyShot and Adobe Photoshop for post-editing. I decided to model a chair and produced two images- one with the chair in a studio environment with minimal background, and the other with the chair in a home environment with more background noise in order to explore different environments and lighting solutions.

FINAL RENDERINGS

process

Since this project was very open-ended I was able to determine a lot of parameters myself. I wanted to create a model that was believable while also creating two images that placed my model in very different environments in order to show my skill set in a variety of areas. I was inspired by this rendering by keyshot influencer Esben Oxholm, and wanted to replicate the believability of the materials.
